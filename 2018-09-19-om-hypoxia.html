Hypoxic stress induces the expression of serum HISP

Stress proteins are induced by various stresses, such as hypoxia, abnormal temperature, and proteomics, etc. HSPs are well-studied stress proteins. Purified protein/peptide was analyzed by FTIR. A significant characteristic absorption peak was detected after exposure to hypoxic stress. It is indicated that a class of special peptide exists in body fluid of AMS patients.
